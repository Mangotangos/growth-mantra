How much does a small business website cost?

Small business websites start at $497 one-time. That includes custom design, local SEO setup, a contact form, and the first 30 days of support. More complex sites with bookings, payments, or member portals are quoted up front.

How long does it take to build a website or app?

Most small business websites launch in 7 to 14 days. Mobile apps typically ship in 4 to 8 weeks. You get a staging link early so you can review and give feedback throughout.

Do you offer fixed-price packages?

Yes. Every website, app, and marketing engagement is fixed-price. The quote is the invoice. No billable hours, no scope-creep surprises, and no hidden fees.

Will I own my website, code, and accounts?

Yes. You own 100 percent of the code, domain, hosting accounts, and social profiles. No lock-in. You can take everything with you and walk away at any time.

What kinds of small businesses do you work with?

Service businesses, local shops, nonprofits, faith-based organizations, cleaners, farms, dairies, trades, and home-service providers. If you serve customers in your community, we can help you grow online.

Do you help with ongoing support after launch?

Yes. Every launch includes 30 days of free support. After that, monthly care plans cover hosting, updates, backups, security patches, and small edits for one predictable fee.
